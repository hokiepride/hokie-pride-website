People who want to contribute to the EC but don't have the time to become full board memebers can instead be on the board as interns! Interns aren't required to attend all meetings like regular EC members, but they can be given simple tasks to complete.

Position Description:

Interns are expected to:

  • Keep up with the general activities of the EC and general body;
  • Assist EC members in managing their duties when they are called on;
  • Be members of the HokiePRIDE community and foster a welcoming environment therein.
