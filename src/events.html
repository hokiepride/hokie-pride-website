In keeping with our mission, HokiePRIDE hosts weekly general body meetings to create a space that elevates voices of our community. They usually have a theme and are informal, so feel free to come and go whenever! Our constitution also tasks us with programming outside of our weekly meetings. These include concerts, comedians, movies, and speakers, as well as volunteer opportunities like The Big Event or Relay for Life.

Have an idea for a special program? Tell us!
